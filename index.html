About Template-driven Forms

Angular template-driven forms provide a simpler, declarative way to build forms using directives like ngModel and ngForm. They rely heavily on templates and two-way data binding.

ngModel enables synchronization between the model and the view, while ngForm manages form state and validation automatically.

Template-driven forms are ideal for simple use cases, while comparisons with reactive forms help developers choose the right approach for their application.
